President Nature Club

Manasseh Mwangi

Nature Club is an Environmental club aimed at conserving our biodiversity, promoting conservation awareness and being voices of reason and change in terms of utilization of our Natural resources. Nature Club incorporates students from all disciplines:

  • Nursing
  • Environmental studies
  • Natural resource management
  • Information science
  • Food science and Nutrition
  • Criminology

Nature club is governed by the principle of environmental solution rather than pollution. We encourage members from all disciplines to join us in making our environment better. The community around us is also part of us as we help in cleaning of our markets and shopping centers, our rivers, etc... We also have tree planting and mentor-ship programs in our local primary and secondary schools, which is our way of giving back to our society. Conservation of our environment is close to us as we lead in promoting awareness on conservation of our mother earth; our tree planting activities both in and out of our university is geared towards increasing the national tree cover to reduce the impacts of climate change regionally, nationally and globally. “Let's Preserve Our Nature

Treasurer Nature Club

Victor Muriithi

Our main aim as nature club is to maintain and conserve our environment. We do engage in many activities that enables us conserve the environment .we do plant trees in our environment to ensure continuity of the available species. We do cleanup activities in and around the school to ensure our environment remains clean. We also have debate activities one’s per week that makes club members active. We also participate in birding activity every Saturday of every week that enables us watch and study various types of birds we have in our environment. Hiking is also part of the activities that we enjoy most as we walk around and watch the beautiful nature of our environment. Long trips gives us chance to move across the parts of the world and learn of various things of nature and the activities in the environments. We wish to have more members in our club so that they can help us conserve and maintain our environment. We also need new members so that we can know more of the activities that we can get in to and can help us maintain a good environment. Being a member of nature club since I joined our university has helped me a lot in the way I interact with the environment. It has enabled me understand the importance of maintaining cleanliness in our environment.  The club’s main source of income mainly from registration and subscription fee, which is paid by the members. Others sources include fund raising activities, volunteers and well-wishers, registration as friends of the club by our alumni. Sale of t-shirts, wristbands also create income to the club. As the treasurer am obliged to, various roles to ensure smooth running of the club Roles include:

  1. Maintain accurate records of financial transaction of the club
  2. To be one of the signatories to all financial transaction of the club
  3. Book keeping and record keeping
  4. Present a financial report during the AGM.
  5. General oversight of the clubs expenditure and budget.
Am also tasked to provide money to finance the club’s activities when needed.
