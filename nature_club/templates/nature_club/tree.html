Tree-Planting and Mentor-ship Subcommittee

Nature club has championed tree planting to various secondary and primary schools. This is through building sustainable and quality relationships with local schools and creating forums for sharing, learning and discussing issues such as environmental protection. Tree planting is also carried out within Karatina University and in the neighboring community.

Tree planting and mentorship has a lot of benefits, some of them include:

  • Tree planting helps the community members learn new techniques of planting trees and the type of trees suitable in their areas
  • Tree planting and mentorship programs makes the young people understand the advantages of tree conservation and disadvantages of tree exploitation hence making them more responsible in environmental conservation.

The importances of trees include

  1. Helps in carbon sequestration
  2. Natural habitats of animals and birds as well as many endangered species.
  3. Clean water Forests provide natural filtration and storage systems of water supply
  4. Temperature control
  5. Flood Control

Kenya has experienced severe deforestation in recent decades. The tree planting events is part of a larger movement aimed at reversing the negative impacts of deforestation for a cleaner and more prosperous Kenya KarU Nature Club we are glad since we are part of the movement.
