About us

Karatina Nature Club Society!

The Nature Club of Karatina University was founded in the year 2010 with the following objectives in mind:
  • Develop and implement programs to enhance environmental awareness and sustainability in line with the university’s performance contract.
  • Exercise social responsiveness by organizing cleanups and tree planting activities within and outside the university.
  • Organizing and participating in internal and external conferences, seminars, symposiums and competitions both national and international related to the conservation of the environment.
  • Sensitizing the university fraternity and adjacent communities on the identification, utilization, and conservation of natural resources available for sustainable development.
  • To liaise with other conservation oriented organizations in management and protection of our limited natural resources.
  • To act as a body through which the university can effectively promote environmental conservation

MEMBERSHIP

Interested in joining the Nature Club of Karatina University? Check out the different membership options and take your pick.

Ordinary Members

All students in the University The membership is recognized upon payment of the Registration Fee of KSHs. 200 only followed by a subscription fee of KSHs. 100 per semester.

Honorary Members

  • Dean of students, Karatina University
  • Club Patron
  • Club assistant patron.
  • Club Auditor.

Friends of the Club

One becomes a friend of the club upon payment of KSHs. 2000 (This category does not include undergraduate students of Karatina University).

Associate Members

These include other societies, organizations and clubs that have related objectives and are willing to work hand in hand with the Nature Club.
